Sentiment-AI Market Bot

Role AI Researcher
Tech Stack
Python NLP Transformers PyTorch
Impact Automated News Analysis

This AI bot monitors financial news and social media sentiment in real-time, using Natural Language Processing (NLP) to gauge potential market movements.

The Challenge

Massive volumes of financial news are published every second. Traders cannot process this manually to identify sentiment shifts that might impact asset prices.

The Solution

We built an NLP pipeline using transformer models to categorize news into bullish, bearish, or neutral sentiments. The system then aggregates these signals per asset, providing a sentiment score that correlates with short-term price volatility.

Methodology

News Ingestion

Designed a real-time scraper for Reuters, Bloomberg, and Twitter using asynchronous Python requests.

NLP Classification

Fine-tuned a FinBERT model to accurately detect financial nuances and sentiment polarities in news headlines.

Signal Generation

Calculated weighted moving averages of sentiment scores to provide trading signals for specific asset classes.

Project Conclusion

The bot successfully automates the first layer of market sentiment analysis, allowing for faster reaction times in algorithmic trading strategies.
